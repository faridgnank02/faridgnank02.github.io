Proximal Policy Optimization pour Contrôle Continu

Support pour reprise d'entraînement et évaluation séparée avec visualisation des performances.

Custom implementation of PPO (Proximal Policy Optimization) algorithm to solve continuous control tasks using PyTorch and OpenAI Gym. Feedforward architecture (2 hidden layers, 64 units, ReLU) for actor-critic. Training on Pendulum-v0 environment with logging of episodic returns, lengths and losses. Support for training resumption and separate evaluation with performance visualization and model checkpointing.

Python PyTorch OpenAI Gym Reinforcement Learning PPO Neural Networks
